Elisavet Kalpaxi is an artist, researcher and lecturer, currently working as a Senior Lecturer in Critical and Historical  Studies at Buckinghamshire New University. She has presented her work at numerous exhibitions and conferences internationally. Her Ph.D. thesis was on ‘Narcissism and Narrativity in Photographic Self-portraiture’ (Goldsmiths, University of London Department of Art, 2012).
